The Goal

Get your pawns to be the last on the grid

  Rules

The game is played on a 6x6 grid.

Both players pawns are randomly placed and fill every tiles at the beginning.

Each turn every pawn of a player will move to the same direction pushing the opponent's pawns if they meet its way.

A pawn will disappear if it moves off the grid.

At the end of each turn, every empty lines and columns of the side will be removed.
